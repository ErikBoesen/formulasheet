\section*{Kinematics}
$$\Delta x=\frac{v_0+v_f}{2}\Delta t$$
$$\Delta x=\frac{1}{2}at^2+v_0\Delta t$$
$$\Delta x=v_ft-\frac{1}{2}at^2$$
$$v_f=v_0+at$$
$$v_f^2=v_0^2+2ax$$
\subsection*{Simple parabolic motion}
$$\Delta x=v_{0x}t$$
$$v_{fx}=v_{0x}$$
$$a_{fx}=0$$

$$\Delta y=v_{0y}t+\frac{1}{2}(-g)t^2$$
$$v_{y}=v_{0y}+(-g)t$$
$$a_{y}=-g$$
